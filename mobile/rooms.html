CHEF'S TABLE

This unique dining experience is perfect for aspiring chefs, the epicurious and those looking for a little entertainment with their meal. The Chef’s Table places you and three guests right in the heart of the action. Chef Shonn and his staff welcome you to their busy kitchen and will answer your questions along the way.

ELEVATOR SHAFT

Perfect for an intimate birthday or anniversary celebration, or perhaps a marriage proposal, this semi-private dining room seats up to four guests. Located off the main dining area, this quiet table is set in a converted elevator shaft.

ROOM ONE

You’ll find this stylishly appointed private room is ideal for all your needs. With seating for up to 20 – it’s perfect for business or pleasure. Room One is equipped with a projector screen and is internet ready. Projector may be available upon request.

ROOM TWO

Characters largest private dining room, ideal for weddings, anniversaries, presentations or corporate affairs, this room features two fireplaces. This comfortable, spacious room can accommodate boardroom seating for up to 30 guests and will hold up to 50 guests when set with round tables. Room two can host stand-up receptions for up to 80 guests. Room two is Internet ready & equipped with a built in LCD projector and drop down screen-ideal for presentations.

ROOM THREE

Perfect for any event, this warm, friendly private room seats up to 14 guests.

ROOM FOUR

Enjoy everything from a working lunch to a special night out in this well-appointed private room. The round table seats up to 12 guests.
