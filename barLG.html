1988 Tenuta San Guido Sassicaia Bolgheri

European viticulture is grounded in traditions; and so, from the traditional Tuscan and Piedmontese varietals of Sangiovese and Nebbiolo, a wine crafted from French grapes grown on Italian soil represented a radical divergence. Created in the 1960s from vines originating in Bordeaux’s Château Lafite Rothschild estate, Sassicaia – the avant-garde “hero wine” of the Bolgheri region – brought Tuscany back into the limelight.

Characters Fine Dining is delighted to stock 16 vintages of Sassicaia, including the exquisite 1988, within our cellar collection of 316 labels and 2831 bottles.

Glenfiddich Rare Collection 1937

In 1937, the year of King George VI’s coronation, cask 843 was laid down in the dark, damp dunnage warehouse at the Glenfiddich Distillery. There it sat for 64 years, maturing and developing into a truly superb Scotch whisky. In October 2001, the Glenfiddich Malt Master declared it an exceptional whisky. Only 61 precious bottles of this treasured spirit were ever filled and sold.

Characters Fine Dining is extremely honoured to have available one bottle of the oldest and most expensive Scotch whisky in the world.
